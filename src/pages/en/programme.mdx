# Program Overview

## Teaching

Classes are taught at the heart of the Paris-Saclay campus by professors from the Lyc√©e International de Palaiseau Paris-Saclay and partner higher education and research institutions.

The program is organized into blocks, spread over three years, with a progressive increase in teaching carried out by higher education and research institutions (ESR):

- **Core fundamentals blocks**: Mathematics (52.5 ECTS) and Computer Science (25.5 ECTS)
- **Applied and specialization domains block**:
  - Data Science and Statistics (34 ECTS) and Social Sciences (16 ECTS)
  - The CPES offers two specialization tracks: a Health track and a Society track (17 ECTS)
- **Transversal block**: English (12 ECTS), research introduction, immersion and projects (9 ECTS) and projects (14 ECTS)
