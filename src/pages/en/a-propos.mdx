## A progressive 3-year structure

The program is designed for a smooth transition to higher education and research. The course location evolves each year:

* **1st year (L1)**: 
    * **80%** of classes at the Lyc√©e International (LIPPS).
    * **20%** at partner institutions (ENS Paris-Saclay, Telecom Paris, etc.).
    * *Objective:* Acquire fundamentals and working methods.
* **2nd year (L2)**:
    * **50%** at LIPPS / **50%** with partners.
    * *Objective:* Beginning of specialization, research projects and immersion.
* **3rd year (L3)**:
    * **30%** at LIPPS / **70%** with partners.
    * *Objective:* Autonomy, final "Capstone" project and preparation for Master's programs.
